about me

I'm currently studying Computer Science and Statistics at the University of Michigan. I'm passionate about tackling society's technical challenges through creativity and teamwork.

I currently serve as President of Theta Tau at my school. My goal is to be the premier engineering student organization on campus.

Outside of class, I'm a jazz saxophonist, and most recently played in the University of Michigan Jazz Lab Ensemble under Dennis Wilson. I also do a little production on the side.

When I'm not doing those two things, I like to let off steam by playing tennis and running 5Ks every now and then.
